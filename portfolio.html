  • Home Lab

    In the Summer of 2023, I managed to get my hands on office computers that would have been recycled.
    I offered to take and fix them so I can learn how to host different types of applications. As of the time of writing, this journey led me to:

    • An incredibly sophisticated video game server hosting platform with Pterodactyl.
    • A cloud storage solution with NextCloud.
    • A fully custom reverse proxy with nginx to handle resolving network traffic through a single dynamic ip. (The one you are connected to!)
    • A place to spin up virtual machines at a whim to build new machines with HyperV 2019. (I'll miss it when it's gone D:)
    • A network-wide ad blocker with AdGuard Home.
    • A custom remote desktop service with RustDesk.
    • And this website!
